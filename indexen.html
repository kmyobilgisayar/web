Kocaeli Vocational School

Department of Computer Technologies

The Department of Computer Technologies aims to train qualified technicians who can work on hardware and software projects needed by the industry. Our graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills to contribute to the workforce.

üí° Purpose

The main purpose of the Computer Technologies Department at Kocaeli Vocational School is to train "technicians" with practical knowledge who can meet the rapidly changing needs of the IT sector. Our program not only transfers theoretical knowledge but also equips students with the ability to transform this knowledge into practical projects and solve real-world problems.

üöÄ Mission / Vision

Our mission is to combine theoretical knowledge with practical applications and train qualified technicians with strong problem-solving skills, ready to adapt quickly to the IT sector. Our vision is to be among the leading vocational school programs in Turkey in the field of computer technologies by following scientific developments and technology both nationally and internationally.
